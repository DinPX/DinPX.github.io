🏠 home

Hello, I go by Din! I'm a pixel artist and the webmaster of this site!

Welcome to my own corner of the internet! It's my space for my creations, thoughts, and interests—practically anything. To see my pixel art visit the gallery!


I made this site because while I'd like to share my works and thoughts, the larger known parts of the internet are pretty unusable. The dystopian landscape is filled with bots, AI, algorithms, Web3, disinformation, and the unnecessary hate. It leaves us humans with no room to breathe, create, and make connections online.

Also I just miss webdev and coding too. Burn out got me for with art.


Speaking of webdev, the site's design was built with Frutiger Aero core in mind, while the layout and desktop theme was inspired with LilithDev!


This site was made possible with Linux Mint, VSCodium, and Librewolf!

NOTE: While there are no NSFW stuff, I write things here with adult audience in mind. There's also few profanity ahead.
