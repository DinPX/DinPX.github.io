My favorite songsss

Here are the songs that are part of me, and made me reach this far in some ways. These are the only songs I'll never remove from myself. This page also list the years I was actively listening, and the covers I listen to (lots of JubyPhonic I tell ya).

My Vocaloid Era (2012-2018)

This is the phase where I got firstly introduced to Hatsune Miku and was then actively listening to their songs! Then I listened to some Kagamine Rin and Len songs, as well as to IA (mostly on Kagerou Project) and Gumi's releases. It's a mix of cute, hopeful, and kinda dark themes.

...Then I was shown with a much darker one by my friend in 10th grade. Despite that, she helped me a lot thanks to her song recommendations that were also uplifting which gave me the push to move forward. After that, I cut ties off her and everyone else, because I always thought that I don't deserve them, and I was really afraid of things on my side. Even so, I still kept the letter from the 'late' present she gave me when I discarded a lot of stuff which were tied to numerous people that were once in my life.
