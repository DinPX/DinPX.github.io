Manifesto

Remember that you create not just for others, but most importantly for yourself too. While following trends is cool, especially if it's one's interests or a part of fandom, creating something unique can be for oneself's appreciation and expression. I believe that the internet wasn't only meant to connect us but also to be a bridge on sharing our own tunes and colors. We express ourselves not just so to 'validate' our purpose or existence, but mainly so that even a single person can here us, and hopefully come to an understanding--especially ourselves as it is also a mirror.
